Registration

All participants are kindly requested to fill out the following registration form (Google Form) and submit it to the workshop secretariat by November 26th, 2018 (AOE). Note that on-site Registration will be available during IWESEP 2018.

Registration Form

Registration fee for IWESEP 2018 is 1,000 Japanese Yen (JPY) for ANY participant. Each registration includes lunch and coffee break. The registration fee will be collected on-site. Only cash payments (no credit cards) can be acceptable.

If you have any trouble filling out the form, please contact with the workshop secretariat. The email address of the workshop secretariat is iwesep2018@is.naist.jp

IWESEP 2018 organizer would like to recommend you to attention to the various co-located events (e.g., APSEC2018).

Dinner

We plan to go to dinner after the workshop. If you are interested in, please check off a box in the form.
